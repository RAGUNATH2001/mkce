Manuscript Submission Format

A submission of paper consist of maximum of 15 pages with 1.5 line spaced, Single Column, Times New Roman, 12 font size and one inch margin on all sides on A4 size. Initial Submission of abstract will be used to review the technical content of the paper. The accepted conference papers will be published in Springer Book Chapter.
